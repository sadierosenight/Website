How to request a commission

  • Fill in the google form here for a watercolor comission.
  • Fill in the google form here for a semi-realism comission.
  • I will review the google form and contact you through the email which you provided.
  • We will communicate about exactly what it is you want (reference images, colours etc).
  • Once we have agreed on what your commission entails, I will send you an invoice where you can also find the Paypal link.
  • Once I received your payment in full, I will start working on your commission as soon as possible.
  • Once I started working on your commission, you can expect the final product within 1 to 3 weeks.
  • There will be an opportunity for review before the product will be finalised.
  • You are allowed to make small changes when you receive the artwork for revision before the final product. Any major changes will result in being charged extra and any additional changes after this revision will also be charged.
